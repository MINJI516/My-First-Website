DEAD POETS SOCIETY

Dead Poets Society is a 1989 American drama film directed by Peter Weir and written by Tom Schulman. The film, starring Robin Williams, is set in 1959 at the fictional elite boarding school Welton Academy,[4] and tells the story of an English teacher who inspires his students through his teaching of poetry.
